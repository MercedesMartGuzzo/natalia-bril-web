LESSONS & COURSES

SINGING

SINGING TECHNIQUE AND PERFORMANCE

GOALS: Develop or deepen your technique, your musical and expressive resources


METHODOLOGY: The technical work focuses on the body since its organization, tone and coordination directly influence the vocal sound. Through the different exercises you will notice movements and parts of your body that you are not aware of, and therefore new breathing and resonance spaces will be enabled. This process will lead you to explore your own voice, accept it, enjoy it and get to know its full potential!


INTENDED FOR: All levels.


MODALITY: Online / Face to face


LANGUAGES: English and Spanish


TANGO SINGING

GOALS:Learn the technical elements and stylistic resources of tango. Explore and sing classic tangos, waltzes and milongas and / or contemporary creations. Discover your own ways within the genre by creating unique versions of the repertoi


CONTENTS:What is Marcato, a syncopation or a pesante? How to phrase on each of the different accompaniment models? What are the specific characteristics of a typical tango vocal performance? Analysis of the lyrics of tangos, waltzes and milongas from classic to contemporary tango. How appropriate the lyrics and interpret them genuinely.


INTENDED FOR: All levels.


MODALITY: Online /Face to face


LANGUAGES:English and Spanish


EAR TRAINING

GOALS

Develop your musical ear and improve your performance skills

Recognize the music that sounds (Rhythm, melody and harmony)


Write the music you create, arrange and compose
Learn and deepen the music theory and notation
Read rhythms and practice sol-fa in different keys
Learn about tonal harmony and counterpoint
Develop yourself with greater ease and skill in music
Appropriate your voice as a study tool

MATERIALS: All the books, audio exercises, songs and every other material to be used during the course will be freely downloaded from the library in my cloud

INTENDED FOR: All levels

MODALITY: Online / Face to face / Asynchronous

LANGUAGES:English and Spanish

VIDEO COURSE

Organize your study rhythm by attending a dynamic video course specially designed to lead your learning process through entertaining activities and exercises. You can choose to take an online synchronous class once every month to ask questions and make sure you are going in the right direction.

Every modalities requires a prior online interview aiming to introduce to each other and to carry out some diagnostic exercises to see your starting point at the different skills.
