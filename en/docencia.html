CLASSES & COURSES

SINGING

SINGING AND INTERPRETATION TECHNIQUE

OBJECTIVES: Develop your technique, your musical and expressive resources


METHODOLOGY: The technical work focuses on the body since its organization, tone and coordination directly influence the sound. Through different exercises you will be able to recognize movements and parts of the body that you cannot imagine, thus enabling spaces for breathing and resonance, among others. This work will allow you to explore your own voice, accept it, enjoy it and know its full potential!


INTENDED FOR: All levels.


MODALITY: Online / Face to face


LANGUAGES: Spanish and English


TANGO SINGING

OBJECTIVES:Know and experience the technical elements and stylistic resources of tango. Explore classic tangos, waltzes and milongas and/or more contemporary creations. Discover your own style within the genre Create unique versions of the repertoire


CONTENTS: What is Marcato, a syncopation or a pesente? How to phrase the different models of accompaniment? What characteristics make a tango voice? The analysis of the lyrics of tangos, waltzes and milongas. Classic tango and current tango. How to make the lyrics your own and interpret them genuinely?


INTENDED FOR: All levels.


MODALITY: Online /Face to face


LANGUAGES: Spanish and English


EAR TRAINING

OBJECTIVES

Develop your musical ear and improve your musical and interpretive skills

Recognize the music that plays (Rhythm, melody and harmony)


Write the music you compose
Know and/or deepen the musical language
Read rhythms and practice melodies in different keys
Learn about tonal harmony
Develop yourself with greater ease and skill in music
Appropriate your voice as a study tool

MATERIALS: All the material to be used is download from my virtual library (bibliography, songs, dictations and more)

INTENDED FOR: All levels

MODALITY: Online / Face to face

LANGUAGES: Spanish and English
